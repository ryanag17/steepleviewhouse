Stay With Us

Welcome to Steepleview House. Whether you come to hike, bike, ski or just relax and enjoy all the beautiful Black Forest has to offer, Steepleview House is a warm and idyllic place to call home during your travels. Choose from our well-equipped apartments or the new private guest rooms. All our guests have access to a patio or balcony, our beautiful beer garden, free WiFi, laundry & BBQ facilities, indoor space for bikes or skis, covered parking for motorbikes, and access to an on-site EV charging station. We hope to see you soon!
